Our suppliers

Enzo Conti

Located in the Abruzzo region in Italy, Enzo and his dog Phonenix has been in the Truffle hunter busissnes for almost a decade.
Their specialty is the black winter truffle also known as Perigord truffle and is mostly found during the colder months of the year.
